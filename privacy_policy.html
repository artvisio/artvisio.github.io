ArtVisio – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how ArtVisio ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or location. The app is used anonymously. We may collect non-personal usage data, including quiz answers, in an anonymized format.

2. Data from Third-Party Services

We use third-party tools to help us operate and improve the app:

  • Firebase – for app performance and crash reporting.
  • RevenueCat – for managing subscriptions.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Improve app features
  • Track performance and fix bugs
  • Manage subscriptions

4. Data Storage

Quiz answers and other non-personal data are stored securely in Firebase. We do not collect or store personally identifiable information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data. Anonymous data may be processed by our third-party providers as part of their service delivery.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personally identifiable data, there is no user account or personal data to modify or delete. If you have questions, you can email us at artvisioai@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for anyone under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We recommend checking this page periodically for updates.
